Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to plant a tree?

Spring and fall are the best times to plant trees because of the milder weather.

How much water do trees need?

Trees generally need about 10 gallons of water per inch of trunk diameter during dry spells.

How do I help conserve trees?

You can conserve trees by supporting tree-planting campaigns, reducing paper waste, and protecting natural forests.
